About Job Seeker Platform

The Job Seeker Platform is a comprehensive web application designed to connect early-career job seekers with recruiters and hiring managers. Built as part of the CS 2340 course project, this platform demonstrates modern web development practices and user-centered design to optimize the recrcuiting experience for all parties involved.

Features

  • Job search and discovery with interactive, advanced filters
  • Profile management for job seekers and recruiters
  • Jop posting and application tracking system
  • Direct communication between candidates and recruiters
  • Administrative tools for efficient and safe platform management

Technology Stack

  • Backend: Django (Python)
  • Frontend: HTML/CSS/Bootstrap
  • Database: SQLite
  • Authentication: Django Auth

Team

This project is being developed by a dedicated team of CS 2340 students committed to creating an excellent platform for job seekers and recruiters alike.
